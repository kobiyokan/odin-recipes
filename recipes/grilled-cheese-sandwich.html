Perfect Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Make a comforting grilled cheese sandwich for a quick and satisfying meal!

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread (any type you prefer)
  • 2 slices of cheese (e.g., cheddar, American, Swiss)
  • 1-2 tablespoons of butter or margarine

Step:

  1. Heat a skillet or griddle over medium heat.
  2. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Place one slice of bread, buttered side down, in the skillet.
  4. Layer the cheese slices on top of the bread in the skillet.
  5. Place the second slice of bread, buttered side up, on top of the cheese.
  6. Cook the sandwich for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  7. Remove the sandwich from the skillet and let it cool for a minute before slicing.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy your gooey grilled cheese sandwich!
