Pizza Recipe

Quick and Easy Pizza

Make a delicious homemade pizza in no time!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made pizza crust
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Assorted toppings (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions)
  • Italian seasoning (optional)

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Place the pre-made pizza crust on a baking sheet.
  3. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the crust.
  4. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.
  5. Add your desired toppings.
  6. If desired, sprinkle some Italian seasoning over the toppings.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  8. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade pizza!
